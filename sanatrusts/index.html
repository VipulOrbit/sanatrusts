Sana College of Education

Affiliated to Karnataka University, Dharwad
Recognised by NCTE , Bengaluru

Sana Trusts has always endeavoured to provide the best of resources, be it human or material, to our students and faculty. Enrichment of student resource has been our priority and the teaching faculty is encouraged to update their technical and management skills. Improving quality is an ongoing process and we strive for excellence in all spheres. The campus is within a five kilometre radius of the Airport, Railway Station, Bus Stand, important places of worship, shopping malls, restaurants and other civic amenities. The institution is connected to all parts of the city through city bus and other transport facilities.
